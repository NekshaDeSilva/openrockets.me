About OpenRockets

Dynamic open-source programming community

OpenRockets is a dynamic open-source community at the intersection of C++, JavaScript, Python, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. We bring passionate coders together to collaborate, innovate, and grow through hands-on experiences.

Through collaborative projects, educational resources, and a vibrant community, we aim to make programming accessible to everyone, from beginners to experienced developers. Our mission is to foster the next generation of innovative programmers and tech enthusiasts.

Education
